About ThriftHood

ThriftHood.cy was born in the streets of Cyprus, where skate culture and urban fashion collide. We're not just another thrift shop - we're a community of street enthusiasts bringing you the dopest second-hand streetwear finds.


Every piece in our collection tells a story, from vintage skate gear to rare streetwear drops. We hunt for the unique, the bold, and the straight-up fire pieces that define street culture.


Join our crew of skaters, artists, and style rebels who believe in sustainable fashion with an edge.
